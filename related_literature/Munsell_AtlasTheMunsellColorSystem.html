Reviewer: grizzelle - favoritefavoritefavoritefavoritefavorite - July 22, 2016
Subject: Color Visualization
After trying to understand Values and Saturation of colors, I came across the Munsell color system. However, I had not found correct references to the correct understanding of values. The original studies are paraphrased (generally ok, but not in depth) in other reference materials. I finally found this site, thank you very much for making all of the Munsell Original studies available to me! What a wonderful gift made by Binney & Smith, Inc., Thank you!
